In the best case of the quick sort algorithm when the pviot divides the array into roughly 2 halves
at every partition. The tree has a height of logn and n comparisons are made at each level
hence t.c is n* logn

In the worst case pivot ends up being either the largest or smallest element. Meaning that the tree
has a height of n and n comparisons are being made hence t.c is n^2
